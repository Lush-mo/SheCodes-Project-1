Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Here is our most sort after Chocolate Chip cookie Recipe.


Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of white sugar
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 400g of unsalted butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Method

  1. melt the butter in a saucepan over low heat till completly melted. Then cool it.
  2. mix the melted btter with the light and brown sugar until thoroughly combined.
  3. add the eggs and mix and the rest of the ingredients to form a dough.
  4. put the cookie dough in the refridgerator to rest for 1 hour.
  5. remove the dough from the refridgerator and portion into small balls.
  6. prepare a baking tray by spraying with oil and a baking sheet. place the dough on the pan and bake at 170Â° for 15 minutes.
  7. when they are baked put the cookies on a wire rack to cool and keep them in an airtight container. eat the cookies within 7 days.
